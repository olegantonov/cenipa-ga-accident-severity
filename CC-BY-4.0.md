## Provenance of the underlying data

The CSV files in `dataset/` are the **public open data** of the Brazilian
Aeronautical Accident Investigation and Prevention Center (CENIPA),
redistributed here for reproducibility:
https://dados.gov.br/dados/conjuntos-dados/ocorrencias-aeronauticas-da-aviacao-civil-brasileira

The derived dataset is de-identified (one row per accident aircraft; no pilot
names or personal identifiers) and was built solely from these public records
and from the publicly available CENIPA final reports.
